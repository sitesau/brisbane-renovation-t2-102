Common questions

How much does a bathroom renovation cost in Brisbane? Most Brisbane homeowners spend $18,000-$25,000 on mid-range bathroom renovations. Budget options start at $10,000 for simple updates, while premium projects with custom features exceed $30,000.

Do I need council approval for a bathroom renovation? Most internal bathroom renovations don't require council approval if they don't alter structural elements or increase floor area. However, always check with Brisbane City Council, especially for heritage-listed properties or major plumbing changes.
